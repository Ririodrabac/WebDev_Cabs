Local Delicacies

A taste of Zamboanga del Sur's culinary heritage.

Taste the Local Flavors

Curacha con Alavar Sauce

Curacha is a species of spanner crab often cooked with a rich, flavorful Alavar sauce, a local specialty. It's a must-try seafood dish in the region.

Satti

A popular breakfast food, Satti consists of grilled meat skewers (typically beef or chicken) served in a sweet and spicy rice porridge. It's a flavorful and unique way to start the day.

Zamboanga White

This frothy, chilled beverage is a delightful mix of condensed milk, lychee, and syrup, topped with ice. It's a refreshing drink perfect for a warm day.

Lokot-Lokot

A crispy, fried delicacy made from ground rice flour and water. The mixture is poured in thin strings into hot oil, creating a noodle-like shape that's folded and served as a sweet snack.

Knickerbocker

A unique and colorful dessert, similar to halo-halo but without shaved ice. It's a delicious mix of fresh fruits, gelatin, and condensed milk, all topped with a scoop of refreshing ice cream.
